feat(realtime): Real-Time Notification Center (Supabase Realtime + Sonner)

Speed-to-lead alerts with zero refresh.

useRealtimeNotifications hook: subscribes to INSERT + UPDATE on `leads`
(filtered to the signed-in user) via Supabase Realtime channels.
  • New decided lead (insert decided, or Processing → decided) → "✨ New Lead"
  • status is/becomes Hot → high-priority "🔥 HOT LEAD"
Dedupes per lead+kind, keeps an in-memory feed (40), exposes unreadCount /
markAllRead / open, and fires a branded Sonner toast on every event.

NotificationBell: header bell with an animated unread badge (solid red dot +
count, only when unreadCount > 0) and a FinTech popover listing recent alerts —
Hot leads in emerald, standard in black; "Mark all read"; click routes to the
lead. Replaces the old static bell.

Sonner <Toaster position="bottom-right"> mounted in the root layout; toasts are
unstyled + fully branded (white card, sky/emerald left rail, black text),
clickable to open the lead.
